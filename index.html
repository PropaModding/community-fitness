Why we exist

Fitness and friendship go hand in hand

We believe staying active isn't just about your body — it's about belonging. Our program brings older Australians together through guided exercise and genuine community.

🏃

Physical health

Regular, tailored movement improves cardiovascular health, balance, and flexibility — reducing the risk of disease and helping you feel your best every day.

🤝

Community connection

Through icebreakers, partner exercises, and post-class catch-ups with tea and snacks, our program is designed to help you make real, lasting friendships.

☀️

Mental wellbeing

Exercise is one of the most powerful tools for mood and mental health. Combine it with social connection and the benefits are profound.

🆓

Completely free

No membership, no fees, no hidden costs. In a time of rising living expenses, we believe access to health and community should never cost a thing.

The program

Two sessions a week, zero cost

Each session runs for about an hour and is led by certified instructors from Get Going Personal Training, who specialise in elderly and rehabilitation fitness. No experience needed.

Session one

Stretch & Yoga

Gentle movement to improve flexibility, posture, and balance. Each class begins with fun social icebreakers like Ball Toss Trivia to get everyone chatting.

Low impact All levels Social warm-up

Session two

Circuit & Cardio

An energising session using light weights, resistance bands, and adapted movement drills — all tailored to your comfort level. Followed by tea and snacks!

Cardio Strength Post-class catch-up

What to expect

Everything's taken care of

From certified instructors to healthy snacks after class — all you need to do is show up.

01

Specialist instructors

Led by Get Going Personal Training, a mobile coaching network specialising in elderly and rehabilitation fitness — all gear included.

02

First aid on site

All instructors hold HLTAID011 First Aid and HLTAID009 CPR certificates. Additional university volunteers provide extra support.

03

Post-class refreshments

Stay for tea, healthy snacks — think veggie platters, hummus, and cheese — and great conversation after every session.

04

Welcome pack

Receive a take-home booklet of gentle home exercises and general health tips to keep you moving every day.

05

Social icebreakers

Every class kicks off with fun group activities like Ball Toss Trivia and This or That — a great way to meet new people.

06

Fully insured

All sessions are covered by corporate public liability insurance through Get Going Personal Training, so you're in safe hands.
