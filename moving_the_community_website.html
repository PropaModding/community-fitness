Why we exist

Fitness and friendship go hand in hand

We believe staying active isn't just about your body — it's about belonging. Our program brings older Australians together through guided exercise and genuine community.

🏃

Physical health

Regular, tailored movement improves cardiovascular health, balance, and flexibility — reducing the risk of disease and helping you feel your best every day.

☀️

Mental wellbeing

Exercise is one of the most powerful tools for mood and mental health. Combine that with social connection and the benefits are profound.

🤝

Community connection

Research shows that nearly one in three older Australians in residential aged care experience loneliness. We're here to change that — one class at a time.


Through icebreakers, partner exercises, and post-class catch-ups, our program is designed to help you make real, lasting friendships in your local area.

The program

Three sessions a week, zero cost

Each session runs for about an hour and is designed to be safe, enjoyable, and appropriate for all mobility levels. No experience needed.

Monday

Stretch & Yoga

Gentle movement to improve flexibility, posture, and inner calm. A wonderful way to start the week.

Low impact All levels

Wednesday

Circuit & Boxing

A fun, energising session using light weights, resistance bands, and adapted boxing drills.

Cardio Strength

Friday

Walk & Connect

A social walk through local parks followed by a guided group activity and post-class tea and snacks.

Outdoors Social

What to expect

Everything's taken care of

From certified instructors to a friendly welcome pack — we've built the program so all you need to do is show up.

01

Certified instructors

Every session is led by qualified fitness professionals experienced in elderly-safe exercise.

02

First-aid on site

Trained first-aid volunteers are present at every session so you can move with confidence.

03

Post-class refreshments

Stay for tea, coffee, and healthy snacks after each session — a great time to chat and unwind.

04

Welcome pack

Receive a take-home booklet of gentle home exercises and general health tips to keep you moving every day.

05

Small group sizes

We cap classes at 10–20 participants so everyone gets personal attention and plenty of room to connect.

06

Accessible venues

Sessions are held at community halls, retirement village courtyards, and local parks — all accessible and close to home.
